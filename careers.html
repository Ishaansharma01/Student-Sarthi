Career Pathways After 12th

Explore the various career options after completing Class 12th across streams and sectors.

Generalized Career Pathways

1. Entrepreneurship

Pursue your own business ideas and ventures, leveraging innovative skills and market understanding.

2. Jobs in Public Sector

After class 12, several government jobs are available:

  • Indian Army: NDA selection, fitness & medical tests, written exam; posts like Soldier General Duty, Technical, Tradesman, Clerk; starting salary Rs. 28,000.
  • Indian Air Force: Agniveer Vayu Exam for posts like Cook, Painter, Carpenter; starting salary Rs. 21,700.
  • Staff Selection Commission (SSC): Jobs as Clerk, Junior Assistant, Data Entry Operator, Constable (BSF, CRPF, CISF); starting salary Rs. 18,000.
  • State PCS Exam: State-level exam; posts like DSP, SDM, Tehsildar, Labour officer through Prelims ‚Üí Mains ‚Üí Interview; starting salary Rs. 50,000.
3. Jobs in Private Sector
  • Sales & Marketing: Sales Executive, Telecaller, Delivery Executive
  • Product Maintenance: Technician Helper, Mechanic Assistant
  • Other Jobs: Data Entry Operator, Office Boy, Call Center/BPO

Stream-Specific Pathways

1. Career Pathways After 12th (Medical)
  • Paramedical & Allied Health: Institutes like Apollo, Fortis, Manipal; jobs as Lab Technician, OT Technician, Radiographer; starting salary Rs. 20,000.
  • Clinical Research Associate: Senior research associate, clinical data manager, research scientist; requires Biology; starting salary Rs. 22,000.
  • Healthcare & Medical Tourism Coordinator: Hospital role requiring communication skills & basic foreign language; salary from Rs. 20,000; future growth in hospital administration.
2. Career Pathways After 12th (Non-Medical Science)
  • Merchant Navy: IMU CET + company exams; roles as Deck Cadet, Engine Cadet; future officer ranks; starting salary Rs. 30,000.
  • Commercial Pilot License: DGCA + flying school exams; pilot in private airlines; starting salary Rs. 1,50,000.
  • Architecture & Design: Private design schools; jobs as Interior Designer, Architect Assistant; B.Arch optional; starting salary Rs. 20,000.
  • Private IT/Engineering Exams with Job Tie-ups: Exams like TCS iON, Infosys Edge, Wipro TalentNext, Cognizant Skill-based hiring; roles as software engineer or analyst; aptitude + interview.
3. Career Pathways After 12th (Commerce)
  • Private Bank PO Exams: Entrance + training; job as Relationship Manager; starting salary Rs. 22,000.
  • Chartered Financial Exams: CA, CFA, ACCA, CIMA; opportunities in Big 4 firms like KPMG.
  • Marketing & Advertising Executive: Marketing assistant, ad campaign executive; starting salary Rs. 20,000; growth to Brand Manager, Marketing Head, CMO.
4. Career Pathways After 12th (Arts & Humanities)
  • Mass Communication & Media Institutes: Jobs as journalist, PR executive, Media Manager; decent salary.
  • NGO & Social Work Assistant: Field worker, project assistant; direct recruitment.
  • Photography & Film Production: FTII entrance or apprenticeships; roles as photographer, assistant, cinematographer; future growth to director or production house owner.
  • Radio Jockey (RJ) & Voice Artist: Skill-based selection via auditions; roles as RJ, dubbing artist, voice artist, voice-over specialist.
